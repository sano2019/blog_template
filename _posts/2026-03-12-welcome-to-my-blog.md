## Why Jekyll and GitHub?

Building a blog doesn't have to be expensive or complicated. By using **GitHub Pages**, we get world-class hosting for $0. By using **Jekyll**, the site loads incredibly fast because it is pre-built as static HTML.

### How to manage this site
1. Log in via `/admin`
2. Write your post in the editor
3. Hit **Publish**

The site will automatically rebuild itself in about 30 seconds.
